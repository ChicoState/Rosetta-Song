Welcome to Rosetta Song!

Rosetta Song is a service that allows users to transfer playlists from one streaming platform to another. Login to the supported services to continue.

1 / 6
Just use Rosetta Song to transfer playlists from one service to another.
2 / 6
To begin, click on the Connect to Services button at the top of the webpage.
3 / 6
You will then be prompted to select the source platform of the playlist you'd like to transfer and the destination platform.
4 / 6
Next, you will be able to select the playlists you'd like to transfer over with a checkmark. To show or hide the songs in each playlist, press the Show/Hide Songs button.
5 / 6
Then, once you're done selecting which playlists you'd like to add, press the transfer button at the bottom of the webpage to intiate the transfer.
6 / 6
If successful, the playlist will have been transfered to your preffered platform where you can listen to it as much as you'd like. Thank you for choosing Rosetta Song!
